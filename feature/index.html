DELIGHTFUL KOREAN CUISINE

Experience the best Korean cuisine right in the heart of the city.

Fish cake

Thinly sliced, seasoned fish paste, boiled or fried, and often skewered or added to soups and stews, providing a savory and slightly sweet flavor.

Kimbap

A popular Korean dish made with steamed rice, vegetables, and sometimes meat, all rolled in seaweed (gim) and sliced into bite-sized pieces.

Kimchi

Fermented vegetables, typically napa cabbage and radish, seasoned with chili powder, garlic, ginger, and salted seafood, giving it a tangy and spicy flavor.

Tteokbokki

A beloved Korean street food, made from chewy rice cakes simmered in a sweet and spicy gochujang-based sauce, often served with fish cakes and boiled eggs.
